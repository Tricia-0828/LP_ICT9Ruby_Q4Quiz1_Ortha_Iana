1.) Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park

Do you like nature adventures? Well then, this is the place for you! Take a ride on a paddle boat and enjoy as you enter a cave that will surely take your breath away.

Entry Fee: P500/person

2.) Samal Island

Being one of the largest resort islands in the Philippines, you surely can't get bored here. A popular place to visit on the island would be Hagmit Falls. Many tourists enjoy there!

3.) Secret Lagoon

Located in El Nido, Palawan, this is a hidden lagoon with its only entrance being a small cave. Once you get in through that cave, you will be astonished by the limestone outcrops shrouded with lush trees.

Entry Fee: P200/person

4.) Hinagdanan Cave

A spooky place that may or may not send shivers down your spine. Bats and spikes are seen in this cave, giving it a rather spooky vibe. However, that's what makes the cave fun to explore! Plus, you can enjoy a refreshing swim in its cool waters.

Entry Fee: P25/person

5.) Coron Island

Being the third largest island in the Calamian Islands north of Palawan, this is a place you must visit. There are caves to explore, and coral reefs to adore. A fun place for adventure-seeking cave explorers or scuba divers.
