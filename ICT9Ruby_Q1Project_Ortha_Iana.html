NOLI ME TANGERE- CHARACTERS

Sources

Picture Name Information
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra
y Magsalin
  • Better known as Ibarra , he is a wealthy young man who came back from Europe after 7 years of studying.
  • He is intelligent and well-respected around his peers.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Crisostomo Ibarra's father who passed away while Crisostomo was in Europe.
  • He was accused of killing a tax collector and was imprisoned.
  • He eventually got sick and died in jail.
  • He was supposed to be burried with the Chinese people, but was disrespectfully thrown into a lake instead.
María Clara de los Santos
y Alba
  • María Clara is well-known in San Diego for her high social status.
  • She is Ibarra's fiancée and the biological daughter of Padre Damaso
Tiya Isabel
  • She is the cousin of Kapitan Tiago and the aunt of Maria Clara.
  • Because Maria Clara's mom passed away, Tiya Isabel filled in for her mom, Donya Pia Alba.
Padre Damaso Verdolagas
  • He is an old, conceited and corrupt Spanish priest
  • Compared to Padre Salvi, he is more brutal. He will not hesitate to use his power to silence others.
  • Thought to be María Clara's godfather, but is later on confirmed to be María Clara's biological father.
  • He's balding
Don Santiago de los Santos
  • Better known as Kapitan Tiago, he is a wealthy, high-ranked land owner in San Diego and in Manila
  • He is legally Maria Clara's father
Padre Bernardo Salvi
  • He is a thin, young, sickly priest, also nicknamed "patay na langaw"
  • He replaced Padre Damaso as San Diego's priest
  • His head looks like an egg on a nest
Sisa
  • A mother with an unending love for her sons, Críspin and Basilio
  • Often emotional as she has been living a harsh life in poverty with her kids and her abusive husband, Pedro.
Basilio
  • The 11-year-old son of Sisa and Pedro, and the older brother of Críspin
  • He and Críspin work as sacristans, although they are unfortunately treated poorly by Padre Salvi and the chief sacristan.
  • Wanting to see his mother again, he found the courage to walk home after the town's curfew,
    getting shot on the forehead while doing so (he lived, don't worry!).
Críspin
  • Pedro and Sisa's 8-year-old son, and the younger brother of Basilio.
  • Just like Basilio, he works as a sacristan and is poorly treated.
  • He was accused of stealing 32 pesos worth of gold.
  • Unfortunately, while at the top of the bell tower, he said something that upset the chief sacristan.
    He was brought downstairs while Basilio was forced to hear a gunshot followed by silence.
Elias
  • A courageous boat pilot who is loyal to the Philippines and to Ibarra.
  • Despite the hardships he's faces, he remains resilient.
  • He courageously jumped into a lake to help Crisostomo flee from the Guardia Civil.
Don Anastasio
  • Also known as "Pilosopo Tasio" or "Tasiong Baliw"
  • He is a smart philosophy student interested in books
  • Many people think he is "weird", but he has already accepted it.
Victorina de los Reyes de Espadaña
  • Better known as Donya Victorina
  • A friend of Capitan Tiago who wants to achieve a higher social status.
Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • He is better known as Don Tiburcio
  • He is a doormat constantly bossed around by his wife.
  • He was thought to be one of the Philippine's best doctors, but barely had experience in medicine.
Donya Consolacion
  • She is an old woman who is really into makeup
  • She is the wife of Alperes.
Alperes
  • He has a sour relationship with Padre Salvi.
  • He and Padre Salvi do not suffer from their wrongdoings, instead, their people suffer..
